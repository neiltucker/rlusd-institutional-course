## **RLUSD Stablecoin Payments for Institutions (Course RUSD-001)**

This comprehensive 1-day, intermediate-level course is designed to onboard institutional partners to the **RLUSD (Ripple USD)** ecosystem. It covers technical implementation, regulatory compliance, and liquidity management using **Bootcamp Mode** for intensive delivery.

This courseware was generated and optimized by [**Courseware Studio**](https://coursewarestudio.ai) and is donated to the Ripple ecosystem to accelerate the adoption of institutional stablecoin rails.

## **🎯 Course Overview**

* **Audience:** Banks, payment providers, fintech firms, and corporate treasury teams.

* **Format:** Instructor-Led or Self-Paced 5-module bootcamp.

* **Duration:** Approximately 1,115 minutes (18h 35m) of total training content.

* **Level:** Intermediate.

## **🚀 Quick Start: Setup & Delivery**

### **1\. Instructor Readiness**

* **Review Timing:** Use the **Trainer Preparation Guide** to review recommended timings for the 5 modules and Capstone labs.

* **Lab Environment:** Provision machines with **Windows 10/11 or Windows Server 2019/2022/2025**.

* **Cloud Setup:** **Microsoft Azure** is the recommended approach for classroom environments to allow fast rollback via snapshots.

* **Hardware:** Minimum 8 GB RAM (16 GB recommended) and 60 GB SSD.

### **2\. Student Path**

* **Prerequisites:** Students should have a basic understanding of payment systems and general awareness of blockchain technology.

* **Assessments:** (Optional) Use the **Pre-Assessment** (Student Handout p. 28\) to baseline knowledge before the course begins.

## **📚 Curriculum Outline**

1. **Module 1:** Introduction to RLUSD and Institutional Infrastructure.

2. **Module 2:** On-Ramps, Off-Ramps, and Liquidity Management.

3. **Module 3:** Compliance Frameworks and Regulatory Expectations.

4. **Module 4:** Operational Use Cases (Remittances, Treasury, and Disbursements).

5. **Module 5:** Organizational Readiness and Cost-Efficiency Assessment.
